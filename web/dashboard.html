📖 How it works — beginner guide

Set it up once, then the bot posts videos to your channel automatically. Just follow these steps.

Step 1 — Sign in
You already signed in with Google. That connects your YouTube channel. ✅

Step 2 — Enable auto-upload
Go to 📺 My Channel → click “Enable auto-upload” → pick your account → press Allow. You should see “Auto-upload ENABLED”. This lets the bot post for you.

Step 3 — Choose your field
Go to 🎬 Create Video → pick a Category (e.g. Science) and Subcategory (e.g. Space). Choose Language and Format. This is your channel's topic — set it once.

Step 4 — Save
Press “Save & generate”. That queues your first video. Check 📋 Requests — it goes queued → building → done, with a “watch” link.

Step 5 — Relax 🎉
The bot now makes videos in your field automatically, a few times a day, and uploads them to your channel. No need to do anything again. You can change your field anytime in Create Video.

Tips
• Videos may be private first — check YouTube Studio → Content.
• Wrong channel connected? Logout and sign in with the correct Google account.
• Want a different topic? Just change Category/Subcategory in Create Video.
